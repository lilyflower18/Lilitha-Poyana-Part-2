We are a non-profit organisation and public benefit organisation based in Uitenhage, Eastern Cape, South Africa. We are dedicated to holistic development and empowerment of young people through our Information and Support Centre.

Executive Summary

Ekhaya Skills Initiative is a registered non-profit organisation from Kwa-Langa, ward 50 at Nelson Mandela Bay's Metropolitan Municipality in the Eastern Cape Province. Ekhaya Skills Initiative deals with the development and empowerment of young people in their holistic development through running an Information and Support Centre. The organisation also facilitates programmes on Social Awareness and Mentorships to provide victim empowerment and personal development.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to contribute positively towards the advancement of humanity through the provision of knowledge-based solutins to economic and social challenges through a centre that provides services for positive social change and community development.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be a first class centre of excellence in advancing information, training and development for needy and suitable communities in the Nelson Mandela Bay.
