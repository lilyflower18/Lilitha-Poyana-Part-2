PROGRAMMES

Pilot Information and Support Centre at Limekhaya High School.

Life Skills & Mentorship Programme at three local primary schools.

Arts Wing, made up of 25 youth artists who are learners at Limekhaya High School.

Feeding Programme which supports 80 children and elderly people 3 days a week.

Community Social Mobilisation Programme, awareness campaigns & dialogues and motivational speaking.

Multi-Media services, video, photography and sound system hire.

BENEFICIARIES

Our direct beneficiaries are the unemployed youth and the community at large, men, women, learners, homeless kids, children in charge of households, vulnerable people, families without an income, who are accessing our packaged information and services.

Other beneficiaries are 17 dedicated volunteers passionate about Community and Youth Development who have completed the ABCD training offered by Ikhala Trust. They have established networks with a number of local stakeholders and companies.
